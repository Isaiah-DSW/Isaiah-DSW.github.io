SILHOUETTE

SILHOUETTE is a 24/7 electronic music radio station with a team of some of the biggest names in the New Zealand electronic music scene hosting shows around the clock. This is my longest-standing (that's still running) and most successful personal project (I started it in late 2018). Check it out! It's got live shows and I even play on it (among other stations).

Tarmas

Tarmas is my Television And Radio Media Automation Software. Yeah, it can do both. At the same time even. This is still in development and isn't released yet. It's totally embeddable in OBS, XSplit & Synergy among other softwares and is designed to be expandable by the user via a comprehensive and powerful API that I would've liked to have seen years ago by the industry leading radio/TV automation software developers.

Sleepyhead

Sleepyhead is a seamless flow of ambient sounds in the form of a livestream to sleep & study to. Based on the original (deprecated but functional) project Sleeeep but with introduced "meandering" which allows for an automated transition between up to 4 (configurable) sounds at a time.

Second.net

Second.net is a re-indexed internet. All domain names are available again and domains can contain special characters like " ". It's pretty much meant to be a more free and secure internet. Websites are able to be on both Second.net AND on the normal internet simultaneously. All you have to do to get on Second.net is to add a script to the head of your page and register it in the directory.

Drift

Drift is the best way to let your audience send you voice messages that you can play out on air live. It drops recordings right into a directory via ftp, has anti-spam protection, and formats the file's meta tags. Perfect for radio stations and streamers.

Funky Cameras

Funky Cameras lets you monitor many of the traffic cameras around New Zealand in near-real time. Due to security and bandwidth the cameras are updated every minute rather than being live video feeds.

Movie Night

Movie Night lets you watch movies and videos with friends synchronized over the internet on individual devices. It's also designed to let you watch movies on your TV without the use of Chromecast. Supports individual private rooms as well as a global room to watch movies with strangers.

Oddball

Oddball is a web-based game that let's you play tag with people using your phone's GPS. Is currently private due to a security risk.

Amongst We

Amongst We is an IRL Among Us spinoff that uses QR Codes to track players and tasks. Cool right? Don't worry, I'm changing the name before the project goes live. Developing in partnership with Valoeghese.
